Shawshank Redemtion

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a drama film based on the 1982 novella "Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption" by Stephen King. The film tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man who is sentenced to life in prison for a crime he did not commit. While in prison, Andy befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and becomes instrumental in bringing hope to the inmates and creating a better life for himself and his fellow prisoners.
