Avatar

Avatar is a 2009 American science fiction film directed by James Cameron. The film is set in the mid-22nd century when humans are colonizing Pandora, a lush habitable moon of a gas giant in the Alpha Centauri star system, in order to mine the precious mineral unobtanium. The story follows a Marine named Jake Sully who is dispatched to Pandora and finds himself torn between following orders and protecting the world he feels is his home.
