Soul

"Soul" is a 2020 American computer-animated fantasy comedy-drama film produced by Pixar Animation Studios. The movie follows Joe Gardner, a middle school music teacher who dreams of being a jazz pianist, as he navigates through life after an accident sends him to a mystical realm where souls develop and gain passions before being sent to newborn children. Through its stunning animation and heartfelt storytelling, "Soul" explores themes of purpose, passion, and the meaning of life, leaving audiences with a profound sense of awe and wonder.
