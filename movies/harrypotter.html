Harry Potter

"Harry Potter" is a series of eight fantasy films based on the seven Harry Potter novels by British author J.K. Rowling. The films chronicle the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The story follows Harry as he learns to harness his magical powers, battles dark wizards, and confronts his own past.
