The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Taylor Jenkins Reid

"The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" by Taylor Jenkins Reid is a beautifully crafted historical fiction novel that tells the story of a Hollywood icon's rise to fame and the many challenges she faced along the way, including a string of tumultuous marriages. The novel is a fascinating exploration of gender and sexuality, and a poignant meditation on the nature of love and ambition.
