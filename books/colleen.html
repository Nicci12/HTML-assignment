It End With Us

Colleen Hoover

"It Ends With Us" by Colleen Hoover is a poignant and thought-provoking novel that explores the complexities of domestic abuse and the difficult choices that victims face. Through the eyes of the protagonist, Lily Bloom, readers witness the devastating effects of domestic violence and the ways in which survivors must grapple with conflicting emotions and loyalties. The novel is a powerful testament to the strength and resilience of survivors, and a call to action for society to do better in supporting victims of abuse.
