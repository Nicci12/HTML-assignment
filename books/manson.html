"The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck"

By Mark Manson

"The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck" by Mark Manson is a self-help book that challenges readers to re-evaluate their priorities and adopt a more realistic and effective approach to self-improvement. With irreverent humor and a refreshing lack of sugar-coating, Manson encourages readers to embrace their flaws and limitations, and to focus their energy on what truly matters in life".
