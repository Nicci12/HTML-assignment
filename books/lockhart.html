We Were Liars

E.Lockhart

"We Were Liars" by E. Lockhart is a gripping young adult novel about a wealthy family's dark secrets and a devastating tragedy that the protagonist, Cadence Sinclair Eastman, struggles to remember. The novel is a powerful exploration of the effects of trauma and family dysfunction, and the unreliable narrator keeps readers on the edge of their seat until the very end.
